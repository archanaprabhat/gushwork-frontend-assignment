Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/8: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Process

German-engineered extruders with precision temperature control ensure consistent wall thickness and molecular alignment throughout.

  • Single & twin-screw extruders
  • Real-time wall thickness monitoring

Controlled Cooling System

Multi-stage water bath cooling preserves dimensional stability and eliminates residual stress from the extrusion process.

  • Vacuum tank sizing
  • Controlled temperature gradient

Precision Sizing

Laser gauging systems verify outer diameter and ovality in real time, ensuring every pipe meets IS:4984 dimensional tolerances.

  • Laser measurement systems
  • Automatic diameter correction

Rigorous Quality Control

100% hydrostatic testing on every pipe batch alongside UV exposure, tensile strength, and impact resistance evaluation.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Third-party certification

Continuous Inline Marking

Inkjet printing applies specification data, batch number, pressure rating, and compliance marks at production speed.

  • IS:4984 marking compliance
  • Traceability via batch codes

Precision Pipe Cutting

Automated planetary cutters create clean, square pipe ends ready for butt fusion jointing without further preparation.

  • ±0.5mm length accuracy
  • Burr-free cut ends

Automated Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, wrapped and loaded with protective end caps. Coils are palletised and shrink-wrapped for transit.

  • Pan-India logistics network
